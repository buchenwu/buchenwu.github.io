Open Positions

We are offering two fully funded PhD positions in our group. The available scholarships include the UM Macao PhD Scholarship (MOP 20,000/month) and a PhD Assistantship (up to MOP 12,500/month). Candidates with strong backgrounds in one or more of the following areas: incompressible/compressible fluid dynamics, heat transfer, fluid-structure interaction, and computational fluid dynamics (CFD) are highly encouraged to apply. Please feel free to contact me for further information or to express your interest.

We are offering one fully funded Postdoctoral Fellow position in our group. The fellowship is the UM Postdoctoral Fellowship, with an annual stipend of MOP 336,000. Candidates with strong expertise in one or more of the following areas: incompressible/compressible fluid dynamics, heat transfer, fluid-structure interaction, and computational fluid dynamics (CFD) are highly encouraged to apply. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested.
